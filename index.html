Show The Way is a youth mentorship program catering to young men in Elmira, NY. Founded by a local native who believed that we, as a community, can and should help these kids understand what it means to be a man in todays' society. Our mission is to connect kids with a positive male role-model, provide a safe nurturing environment for them to explore who they are and what they want to do with their lives, and help guide them as they become men.
